Propagation direction is dentermined by two paramter thetaz and phiz. They are just polar and azimuthal angle in spherical coordinatte, so propagation direction is . For the definition of L\+CP, and R\+CP I choose view from source. Therefore, for left or right hand rule, direction of thumb is same as propagtion direction (out of source).
