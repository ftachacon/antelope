Constructor for pulse,. 

Propagation direction is dentermined by two paramter thetaz and phiz. They are just polar and azimuthal angle in spherical coordinatte, so propagation direction is $ (\sin\theta_{z}\cos\phi_{z}, \sin\theta_{z}\sin\phi_{z}, \cos\theta_{z})$. For the definition of L\+CP, and R\+CP I choose view from source. Therefore, for left or right hand rule, direction of thumb is same as propagtion direction (out of source). To doing standard 2d calculation (x-\/y plane), set thetaz = 0 and phiz = 0, or just omit them from the constructor.
